About Us

UQ Artifical Intelligence and Machine Learning Club (UQ AIML) focuses on data science, machine learning and artifical intelligence. UQ AIML, formerly known as UQ Statistics Society, has existed as part of UQ’s Student Association of Science Societies (SASS) for a number of years. This year we aim to replicate the success in these areas and build on the society’s reach and influence. We will bring a renewed focus on industry networking, and expand the scope of our activities to modern applications, such as data science and machine learning.
